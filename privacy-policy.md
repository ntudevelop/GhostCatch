# GhostCatch 👻 — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 31, 2026  
**Entity:** GhostCatch ("we," "our," or "us")

---

## 1. At a Glance: Our Privacy Philosophy
GhostCatch is built on the principle of **Data Minimization**. We believe your financial habits are your business.

* **Zero-Knowledge Architecture:** Your subscription data is stored locally on your device.
* **No Central Servers:** We do not host databases containing your personal subscription lists.
* **On-Device Processing:** Features like OCR (scanning) happen locally; your images are never uploaded to our servers.

---

## 2. Information Collection and Usage
We distinguish between the data you manage (which we cannot see) and the technical data required to run the app.

### A. Data You Manage (Local Only)
This information stays on your device and is never transmitted to GhostCatch.
* **Subscription Details:** Names, costs, renewal dates, and categories.
* **Customization:** Reminders, custom categories, and theme preferences.
* **Media:** Receipts or screenshots scanned via OCR.

### B. Data We Process (Functional)
Necessary for app performance and premium features.

| Category | Purpose | Data Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Purchases** | To validate "GhostCatch Premium" status. | Transaction IDs via Apple/Google. |
| **Stability** | To identify and fix software bugs. | Anonymous crash logs (Opt-in). |
| **Analytics** | To understand app version compatibility. | Device type, OS version, App version. |

---

## 3. Third-Party Integrations
To provide a seamless experience, we use a limited number of trusted service providers.

* **App Store Platforms (Apple & Google):** Handle all payment processing. We never see your credit card or bank details.
* **RevenueCat:** Used to manage cross-platform subscription logic. They receive anonymous identifiers to verify if you have purchased Premium features. [View RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

---

## 4. Data Security & Storage
> **Security Note:** Because your data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device, the security of your data is tied to the security of your physical device.

* **Local Encryption:** We utilize industry-standard encryption for local storage.
* **No Cloud Sync:** GhostCatch does not automatically sync your data to a cloud. If you wish to back up your data, you must use the manual Export (CSV/JSON) feature.
* **Data Portability:** You have the right to export or delete your data at any time via the "Settings" menu.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Controls
You maintain absolute control over your information:
* **Access & Portability:** View and export your data directly within the App.
* **Erasure:** Deleting the App or using the "Delete All Subscriptions" button permanently removes all local data.
* **Notifications:** Manage or disable renewal alerts through system settings.
* **Tracking:** We do not track you across third-party websites or apps.

---

## 6. International & Children’s Privacy
* **Children:** GhostCatch does not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13.
* **International:** Your data remains on your device regardless of your residency. Purchase processing adheres to the regional standards of the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
